
### 1. Performances & Shows 
| **Hebrew Word**  | **Transcription** | **Meaning** | **Context/Usage** |  
|-----------------|----------------|------------|-----------------|  
| **址爪指旨指**  | *hatsag谩* | Play, presentation | **Theatrical performances or formal presentations** |  
| **止驻指注**  | *mof谩* | Show, performance | **General entertainment event, often concerts** |  
| **止驻指注指**  | *hofa'谩* | Performance, appearance | **Live performance by an artist or public appearance** |  
| **址植侄**  | *machaz茅* | Play, drama | **A written script or dramatic work** |  


**驻注 vs. 驻注:**

*驻注* is more general (concerts, performances), while *驻注* is more personal (a singers live performance).


### 2. Exhibitions & Displays 硷锔 
| **Hebrew Word**  | **Transcription** | **Meaning** | **Context/Usage** |  
|-----------------|----------------|------------|-----------------|  
| **转址旨注植专旨指**  | *ta'aruch谩* | Exhibition | **Art, science, or trade fair exhibits** |  
| **转旨址爪旨指**  | *tatsug谩* | Display, showcase | **Product displays, store showcases, or tech presentations** |  
| **执爪职指旨**  | *mitsg谩* | Multimedia exhibit | **Museum displays or interactive exhibits** |  


**转爪 vs. 转注专:**

*转爪* is more commercial (product displays), while *转注专* is for **exhibitions (art, science, etc.)**.  


### 3. Demonstrations & Presentations 
| **Hebrew Word**  | **Transcription** | **Meaning** | **Context/Usage** |  
|-----------------|----------------|------------|-----------------|  
| **址职指旨指**  | *hadgam谩* | Demonstration, example | **Explaining or showing how something works** |  
| **址爪旨指**  | *mats谩g* | Display, visual representation | **Legal arguments, digital presentations, informational displays** |  
| **址爪旨侄侄转**  | *matseget* | Slideshow, presentation | **PowerPoint or educational/business presentations** |  


**爪转 vs. 爪:**

*爪转* is specifically a **slideshow presentation**, while *爪* is a **broader visual display**.


### 4. Artistic & Conceptual Installations
