# Topic: to Deal, Cope, Manage, Handle

| **Hebrew**    | **Transcription** | **Preposition** | **Meaning**         | **Example**                                          |
| ------------- | ----------------- | --------------- | ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **לְהִתְמוֹדֵד**  | *lehitmodéd*      | **עִם**          | to cope; to deal with (a difficulty/challenge)     | **אני מתמודד עם לחץ.** – I’m coping with stress.     |
| **לְהִסְתַּדֵּר**   | *lehistadér*      | **עִם**, **בְּלִי** | to get along, handle, manage                       | **אל תדאג, אני אסתדר.** – Don’t worry, I’ll manage.  |
| **לְהִתְגַּבֵּר**   | *lehitgabér*      | **עַל**           | to overcome                                        | **הוא התגבר על הפחד.** – He overcame the fear.       |
| **לְטַפֵּל**     | *letapél*         | **בְּ**            | to take care of; to handle (a matter/task/problem) | **אני מטפל בזה היום.** – I’ll take care of it today. |
| **לְנַהֵל**     | *lenahél*         | **אֶת**           | to manage/run (a project, process, team)           | **היא מנהלת את הפרויקט.** – She manages the project. |
