# Topic: to Make, Produce, Generate, Create

## 1. General making

| **Hebrew**      | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                  | **Example**                                          |
| --------------- | ----------------- | ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **לַעֲשׂוֹת**   | *la’asót*         | to do; to make               | **אני עושה קפה.** – I’m making coffee.               |
| **לְהָכִין**    | *lehachín*        | to prepare; to make ready    | **אני מכין ארוחת ערב.** – I’m preparing dinner.      |
| **לְהַרְכִּיב** | *leharkív*        | to assemble; to put together | **הוא הרכיב שולחן חדש.** – He assembled a new table. |

## 2. Creating something new

| **Hebrew**    | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                       | **Example**                                                                           |
| ------------- | ----------------- | --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **לִיצוֹר**   | *litzór*          | to create                         | **היא יוצרת מוזיקה מקורית.** – She creates original music.                            |
| **לִבְרוֹא**  | *livró*           | to create (lofty, often divine)   | **לפי המסורת, אלוהים ברא את העולם.** – According to tradition, God created the world. |
| **לִבְנוֹת**  | *livnót*          | to build; to construct            | **הם בונים בית חדש.** – They are building a new house.                                |
| **לְהָקִים**  | *lehakím*         | to establish; to found; to set up | **הם הקימו חברה חדשה.** – They established a new company.                             |

| **לְחַבֵּר**  | *lechabér*        | to compose; to write; to create   | **הוא חיבר שיר יפה.** – He composed a beautiful song.                                 |

## 3. Producing

| **Hebrew**      | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                                   | **Example**                                                                   |
| --------------- | ----------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **לְיַיצֵּר**    | *leyatzér*        | to produce; to manufacture                    | **המפעל מייצר מכוניות.** – The factory produces cars.                         |
| **לְהָפִיק**    | *lehafík*         | to produce; to generate; to derive            | **אפשר להפיק חשמל מרוח.** – It is possible to generate electricity from wind. |
| **לְהָנִיב**    | *lehanív*         | to yield; to produce (fruit, profit, results) | **ההשקעה הניבה רווחים יפים.** – The investment yielded nice profits.          |
| **לְהַצְמִיחַ** | *lehatzmíach*     | to grow; to generate; to foster               | **הפרויקט הצמיח רעיונות חדשים.** – The project generated new ideas.           |
