# Topic: Section, Fragment

| **Hebrew** | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                | **Example**                                                                                       |
| ---------- | ----------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **חֵלֶק**    | *chelek*          | part, portion              | **זה חלק מהתוכנית** (*ze chélek meha-tochnít*) – That’s part of the plan                          |
| **קֶטַע**    | *keta*            | section, excerpt, segment  | **קראתי קטע מהספר** (*karatí kéta meha-séfer*) – I read an excerpt from the book                  |
| **מִקְטָע**   | *mikta*           | segment (technical)        | **מקטע הכביש הזה סגור** (*mikta ha-kvísh ha-zé sagúr*) – This road segment is closed              |
| **פֶּלַח**    | *pelach*          | slice (food); segment (economy)  | **פלח תפוז** (*pelach tapúz*) – slice of orange                                             |
| **מִגְזָר**   | *migzar*          | sector (society, economy)  | **המגזר הפרטי** (*ha-migzár ha-pratí*) – the private sector                                       |
| **פֶּרֶק**    | *perek*           | chapter; episode; section  | **הפרק הבא מעניין מאוד** (*ha-pérek ha-ba me’anyén me’ód*) – The next chapter is very interesting |
| **חֲתִיכָה**  | *chaticha*        | piece, slice, chunk        | **חתיכה גדולה של עוגה** (*chaticha gdolá shel ugá*) – a big piece of cake                         |
| **פְּרוּסָה**  | *prusa*           | slice (thin)               | **פרוסת לחם** (*prusát léchem*) – a slice of bread                                                |
| **נֵתַח**    | *netach*          | cut/portion; share/portion | **נתח בשר** (*nétach basár*) – a cut of meat                                                      |

## Notes

* **Text/media:** קטע (short excerpt) vs פרק (formal chapter/episode)
* **Technical:** מִקְטָע (defined segment)
* **Business/society:** מִגְזָר (sector), פלח (market segment), נתח (market share)
* **Food/physical pieces:** חתיכה (chunk), פרוסה (thin slice), פלח (wedge), נתח (cut)
