# Topic: Joy, Pleasure

### Joy & Pleasure

| **Hebrew**  | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                                                  | **Example**                                                                                   |
| ----------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **שִׂמְחָה**    | *simchà*          | joy, gladness (the feeling)                                  | **יש שמחה בבית.** (*yesh simcha ba-bayit*) – There is joy in the house.                       |
| **אֹשֶׁר**     | *osher*           | happiness, well-being (a more lasting state)                 | **היא מאחלת לו אושר.** (*hi me’achelet lo osher*) – She wishes him happiness.                 |
| **הֲנָאָה**    | *hana’à*          | enjoyment, pleasure (from doing/experiencing something)      | **אני מקבל הנאה מהקריאה.** (*ani mekabel hana’a me-ha-kri’a*) – I get enjoyment from reading. |
| **תַּעֲנוּג**   | *ta’anúg*         | delight, a treat, great pleasure (often “what a treat”)      | **הקינוח היה תענוג.** (*ha-kinuach haya ta’anug*) – The dessert was a delight.                |
| **כֵּיף**     | *kef*             | fun (informal, everyday)                                     | **היה כיף אתמול.** (*haya kef etmol*) – It was fun yesterday.                                 |
| **עֹנֶג**     | *oneg*            | pleasure, bliss (more elevated; also “a pleasure to…”)       | **זה היה עונג לפגוש אותך.** (*ze haya oneg lifgosh otcha*) – It was a pleasure to meet you.   |
| **גִּיל**     | *gil*             | joy, exultation (poetic/elevated)                            | **הוא קפץ מגיל.** (*hu kaftz mi-gil*) – He jumped for joy.                                    |
| **שָׂשׂוֹן**    | *sason*           | gladness, rejoicing (biblical/poetic; often in set phrases)  | **קול ששון ושמחה.** (*kol sason ve-simcha*) – A sound/voice of rejoicing and joy.             |

### Entertainment & Leisure

| **Hebrew**    | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                                  | **Example**                                                                                                               |
| ------------- | ----------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **בִּידוּר**    | *bidúr*           | entertainment (shows, media)                  | **אני מחפש בידור קליל לערב.** (*ani mechapes bidur kalil la-erev*) – I’m looking for light entertainment for the evening. |
| **שַׁעֲשׁוּעַ**    | *sha’ashu’à*      | amusement, playful enjoyment                  | **זה היה שעשוע נחמד.** (*ze haya sha’ashu’a nechmad*) – It was a nice bit of amusement.                                   |
| **בִּילּוּי**    | *bilúy*           | recreation, entertaintment (the event itself) | **יצאנו לבילוי בעיר.** (*yatzanu le-biluy ba-ir*) – We went out in the city for an outing.                                |
| **פְּנַאי**     | *pnai*            | leisure time (free time as a concept)         | **אין לי הרבה פנאי השבוע.** (*ein li harbe pnai ha-shavua*) – I don’t have much free time this week.                      |
