# Topic: Clearly, Explicitly

### Clearly

| **Hebrew**   | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                                                | **Example**                                                                                       |
| ------------ | ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **בְּבֵירוּר**   | *beveirúr*        | clearly, plainly, evidently                                | **רואים בבירור את ההבדל.** (*ro’im beveirur et ha-hevdel*) – You can clearly see the difference. |
| **בִּבְהִירוּת**  | *bivhirút*        | clearly, with clarity (often about explaining/writing)     | **הוא הסביר את זה בבהירות.** (*hu hisbir et ze behirut*) – He explained it clearly.              |

### Explicitly

| **Hebrew**   | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                                       | **Example**                                                                                             |
| ------------ | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **בִּמְפוֹרָשׁ**   | *bemeforash*      | explicitly, expressly (often rules/instructions)  | **זה נאסר במפורש.** (*ze ne’esár bemeforesh*) – This is explicitly forbidden.                           |
| **בְּפֵירוּשׁ**   | *beferúsh*        | explicitly, in so many words, clearly stated      | **הוא אמר בפירוש שהוא לא בא.** (*hu amar befeyrush she-hu lo ba*) – He explicitly said he’s not coming. |
| **בְּגָלוּי**    | *begalúy*         | openly, explicitly (said openly, not hidden)      | **היא אמרה את זה בגלוי.** (*hi amra et ze begaluy*) – She said it openly.                               |
