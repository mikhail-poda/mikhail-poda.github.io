# Topic: Particularly, Especially

| **Hebrew**   | **Transcription** | **Meaning**                              | **Note**                                   | **Example**                                                                                  |
| -------------| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **בִּמְיוּחָד**   | *bimyuchad*       | especially, particularly                 | “extra emphasis”, just highlights something  | **אני אוהב מוזיקה קלאסית, במיוחד בטהובן** – I like classical music, especially Beethoven |
| **בְּיִיחוּד**   | *beyichúd*        | particularly, especially                 | formal version of  **בִּמְיוּחָד**             | **המאמר חשוב, בייחוד הפרק האחרון** – The article is important, particularly the last chapter |
| **בִּפְרָט**     | *bifrat*          | in particular, especially                | specific details from a larger group       | **אני אוהב ספרים, ובפרט ספרי היסטוריה** – I like books, especially history books             |
| **בְּעִיקָּר**    | *be‘ikár*         | mainly, mostly, especially (in essence)  | main vs. secondary                         | **אנחנו מדברים עברית, ובעיקר בבית** – We speak Hebrew, mainly/especially at home             |
| **דַּוְקָא**     | *dávka*           | specifically, *of all things*, “of all…” | colloquial, surprising/unexpected choice   | **הוא בחר דווקא בי לעבוד איתו** – He chose specifically me (of all people) to work with him  |
